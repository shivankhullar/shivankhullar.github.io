Home

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Toronto. I'm working with Prof. Chris Matzner and Prof. Norman Murray. My research focuses on star forming regions in our universe. Star formation is a multi-scale multi-physics problem, and I use numerical simulations run on supercomputers to answer Nature's secrets. I finished my undergraduate studies from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science - Pilani, Goa Campus. majoring in Physics. In the past, I have also been interested in the CMB, Reionization and Cosmology.
